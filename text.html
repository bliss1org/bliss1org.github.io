Values that are important to me is gratitude, putting in your all, and self-respect. 

On top of that, I value respecting elders, being safe with your action, and empathy for the world. 

These values are important to me in a variety of deep-rooted, fundamental reasons. 

Gratitude is important because it is a sign of love, and I believe love is the only thing that is real, 

Not showing gratitude or understanding it means taking everything for granted. 

Putting in your all is important as a sign of effort, and love is the opposite of a lack of interest 

- by putting in your all you increase your self-esteem 

in where you want to go and helps straighten your priorities. 

Self-respect is crucial because with no self-respect then what is the point of living, 

and to live is to respect the vessel you are in - 

by not letting others take advantage of you, 

by putting effort into life, and putting the respect into yourself 

you recognize everyone else deserves 

the same respect or understanding how they respect you better. 

Respecting elders is significant because they offer the wisdom of life, 

how it may turn good and bad, what makes it worth living, 

and the beauty in the moments that give you lifeblood. 

Being safe with your action is important because if you don't find the action safe, 

you can understand the action that could put you in an uncomfortable or unforgiving situation 

which personally I'd rather avoid to live my life peacefully. 

Empathy for the world is crucial because 

understanding that every person from every walk of life has pain, 

we can help each other to seek a better future for all our benefits. Thanks for reading.\\
